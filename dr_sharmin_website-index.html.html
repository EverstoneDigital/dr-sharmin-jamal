About Doctor

Dr. Syeda Sharmin Jamal

ডাঃ সৈয়দা শারমিন জামাল

Dr. Syeda Sharmin Jamal is a distinguished ENT and Head-Neck Surgeon serving as Assistant Professor in the Department of Otolaryngology & Head-Neck Surgery at Bangladesh Medical University (formerly BSMMU / PG Hospital), Dhaka. With over fifteen years of dedicated practice, she has earned a reputation as one of Bangladesh's most accomplished specialists in her field.

Her academic foundation includes MBBS from the University of Dhaka and DLO from BSMMU, enriched by advanced training in Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery at Singapore's National University Hospital, and specialised education in Head, Neck & Ear Surgery across the USA, UK, and Korea.

Deeply committed to accessibility, Dr. Jamal now offers online consultations so patients across Bangladesh — including those outside Dhaka — can receive expert ENT advice from home via video call, WhatsApp, or phone.
